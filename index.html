🙏 + 🙂‍↕️ = Sawasdee

What is Sawasdee? 🇹🇭

"Sawasdee" is the traditional Thai greeting, accompanied by the "wai" gesture - a slight bow with palms pressed together. This respectful greeting is an essential part of Thai culture and hospitality.

How to Use the Emoji 📱

Simply combine these two emojis:

  • 🙏 Folded Hands
  • 🙂‍↕️ Face with Vertical Movement

Use this combination to express the traditional Thai greeting in your digital communications!

Cultural Significance ✨

The Sawasdee emoji combination represents:

  • Respect and courtesy
  • Traditional Thai values
  • Warm welcomes and greetings
  • Cultural appreciation
